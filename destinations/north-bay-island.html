North Bay Island

About North Bay Island

Coral capital just offshore

If you want to experience the Andamans' famous coral reefs without venturing far from Port Blair, North Bay Island is the answer. Just a 30-minute boat ride from the Water Sports Complex at Port Blair, this small island is packed with water activities — snorkelling over vibrant reefs, sea walking on the ocean floor, glass-bottom boat rides, and even scuba diving for beginners.

Activities

What you need to know

Four quick things to keep in mind as you plan your visit.

01

Snorkelling

Colourful coral reefs in shallow water — equipment provided, suitable for beginners.

02

Sea Walking

Walk on the sea bed wearing a special helmet — no swimming skills needed.

03

Glass-Bottom Boat

See the coral and marine life without getting wet — perfect for families with small children.

04

Scuba Diving

Introductory dives for first-timers with PADI-certified instructors.
