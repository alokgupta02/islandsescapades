Our story

Started by people who know every tide here

Islands Therapy is a locally-rooted travel company based in Port Blair, specialising in bespoke Andaman & Nicobar Islands holidays. With deep knowledge of every island, beach, and hidden cove, we design journeys that go beyond the ordinary.

Whether you seek the romance of a secluded sunset at Radhanagar Beach, the thrill of scuba diving at Havelock, or the serenity of a private yacht cruise — we handle every detail so you can simply savour the moment.
