Adventure Explorer Package

Six days of scuba, rainforest treks, mangrove kayaking and underwater worlds most people only see in documentaries.

About this trip

Dive, trek, paddle, explore

If turquoise waters and tropical forests get your adrenaline pumping, this is your package. Dive into the Indian Ocean alongside manta rays and sea turtles, trek through the dense rainforests of Mount Harriet, paddle through bioluminescent mangrove creeks, and explore underwater worlds that most people only see in documentaries.

Day by day

Your itinerary

A flexible draft — every day can be tuned to your pace and interests.

01

Port Blair

Arrival, Cellular Jail visit, evening trek at Chidiya Tapu (Bird Island) for a panoramic sunset.

02

Mount Harriet & Madhuban

Full-day guided trek through Mount Harriet National Park to Madhuban Beach — 16 km through pristine rainforest with exotic bird sightings.

03

Havelock: Scuba Day

Ferry to Havelock. Introductory or certified scuba dive at sites like Aquarium, Lighthouse, or Jackson's Bar. Afternoon snorkelling at Elephant Beach.

04

Havelock: Kayak & Explore

Sunrise mangrove kayaking expedition. Afternoon free for Radhanagar Beach. Optional night kayaking through bioluminescent waters.

05

Neil Island

Ferry to Neil. Snorkelling at Bharatpur Beach. Cycling tour of the island. Visit the Natural Rock Bridge at sunset.

06

Departure

Return to Port Blair. Last-minute shopping and airport transfer.
