About this trip

Memories for every age

The Andamans are a paradise for families — shallow turquoise lagoons safe for children, fascinating history at the Cellular Jail, and wildlife encounters that ignite young imaginations. Our Family Fun Package covers Port Blair, Havelock Island, and a day trip to Ross and North Bay Islands, with activities tailored for all ages.

Day by day

Your itinerary

A flexible draft — every day can be tuned to your pace and interests.

01

Arrival in Port Blair

Airport pickup. Visit the Cellular Jail National Memorial and the evening Light & Sound Show — a moving experience for the whole family.

02

Ross Island & North Bay

Morning boat to Ross Island (historic British ruins with friendly deer). Then North Bay for glass-bottom boat rides and coral viewing — kids love it.

03

Port Blair to Havelock

Ferry to Havelock. Afternoon at Radhanagar Beach with sandcastle building. Seafood dinner at a beachside restaurant.

04

Havelock Adventures

Elephant Beach visit with banana boat rides and snorkelling. Mangrove kayaking suitable for families. Evening bonfire on the beach.

05

Departure

Morning ferry to Port Blair. Souvenir shopping at Aberdeen Bazaar. Airport transfer.
