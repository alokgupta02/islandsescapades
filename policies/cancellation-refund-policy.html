Cancellation & Refund

Plans change — here's exactly how cancellations, reschedules, and refunds work when you book with Ocean Therapy.

Last updated · 2026

Cancellation by Traveller

We understand plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair while covering the commitments we make to our service partners on your behalf:

30+ days before departure: Full refund minus ₹1,000 processing fee per person.

15–29 days before departure: 50% refund of total package cost.

7–14 days before departure: 25% refund of total package cost.

Less than 7 days before departure: No refund.

All cancellation requests must be submitted in writing via email to hello@islandstherapy.com. The cancellation date is the date the email is received.

Cancellation by Ocean Therapy

In the rare event that we need to cancel a trip due to unforeseen circumstances (natural disasters, government restrictions, severe weather, minimum group size not met), you will receive a full 100% refund or the option to reschedule at no additional cost.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed within 7–10 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Bank processing times may add 3–5 additional business days for the amount to reflect in your account.

Non-Refundable Items

The following are non-refundable regardless of cancellation timing: flight tickets (managed by airlines directly), travel insurance premiums, visa/permit processing fees, and any services already rendered.

Date Changes & Rescheduling

Date changes requested 15+ days before departure are accommodated free of charge (subject to availability). Changes within 15 days incur a ₹2,000 per person rescheduling fee. Only one date change is permitted per booking.

No-Show Policy

Travellers who fail to arrive at the departure point without prior cancellation notice forfeit the entire booking amount. No refunds are issued for no-shows.

Partial Usage

No refunds are given for unused portions of the package (missed activities, early departure, unused hotel nights) unless caused by a medical emergency supported by documentation.
