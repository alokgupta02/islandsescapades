Jet Skiing

Powerful machines, warm turquoise water, and stunning island views — a fast, exhilarating way to feel the Andaman Sea.

About this experience

Adrenaline on the Andaman Sea

Jet skiing is one of the most popular adrenaline-pumping activities in the Andamans. Hop onto a powerful jet ski and race across the warm, turquoise waters with the sea breeze in your face and stunning island views all around. Whether you ride solo or with a guide, it's a thrilling way to experience the Andaman Sea.

Good to know

Where to jet ski

Practical info to help you decide if this is right for your trip.

01

Rajiv Gandhi Water Sports Complex

Port Blair's primary water sports hub near Andaman Teal House. Well-maintained equipment and trained instructors.

02

Corbyn's Cove Beach

A scenic bay with calmer waters. Popular with tourists staying in Port Blair.

03

Elephant Beach, Havelock

Ride alongside coral reefs in the clearest waters. Premium experience with stunning backdrop.
