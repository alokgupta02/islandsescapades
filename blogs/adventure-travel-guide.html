Adventure travel guide to Andaman

For the thrill-seekers and explorers chasing more than just a beach holiday.

If your idea of a holiday involves more adrenaline than sunbathing, the Andaman Islands will not disappoint. Beneath the paradise veneer lies one of India's best adventure playgrounds — underwater caves, live volcanoes, dense jungle treks, and open-ocean dive sites. Here's your complete adventure guide.

Underwater Adventures

Scuba diving is the star attraction. Havelock's dive sites (Aquarium, Lighthouse, Dixon's Pinnacle) offer encounters with manta rays, reef sharks, and Napoleon wrasse. For certified divers, Cinque Island is the holy grail — remote, pristine, and teeming with life. Sea walking at North Bay and night snorkelling are gentler alternatives that still feel extraordinary.

Trekking & Nature

Saddle Peak in Diglipur (732 m) is the highest point in the Andamans and requires a full-day trek through evergreen forest. The Mount Harriet trail from Madhuban (16 km) passes through old-growth trees and ends with panoramic views. For a shorter option, the Chidiya Tapu nature trail is 2 km of birdwatching bliss.

Water Sports Bucket List

Parasailing at Corbyn's Cove (50–80 m above the sea). Jet skiing across turquoise bays. Kayaking through bioluminescent waters at Havelock after dark. Speed-boating to remote islands. Banana boat and tube rides at Elephant Beach. The Andamans are stacked with options.

The Volcano Experience

Barren Island is India's only active volcano. While you can't land on the island, boat tours circle it and offer views of the volcanic landscape and surrounding marine life. It's a surreal experience — swimming in warm, mineral-rich waters near an active geological hotspot.

5-Day Adventure Itinerary

Day 1: Port Blair — jet skiing and parasailing at Rajiv Gandhi Water Sports Complex. Day 2: Ross and North Bay combo — scuba and sea walk. Day 3: Havelock — deep dive at Dixon's Pinnacle (certified) or Discover Scuba (beginner). Day 4: Elephant Beach water sports + bioluminescent kayaking at night. Day 5: Trek to Elephant Beach + return to Port Blair.
