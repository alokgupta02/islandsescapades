Budget travel tips for Andaman

Experience paradise without breaking the bank — smart planning for a sub-₹15,000 island holiday.

You don't need a luxury budget to enjoy the Andaman Islands. With smart planning, you can experience world-class beaches, incredible snorkelling, and island life for well under ₹15,000 per person for a 4-night trip. Here's how.

Getting There Affordably

Flights to Port Blair from cities like Chennai, Kolkata, and Delhi are the only way in (unless you take a 3-day ship from Chennai or Kolkata, which costs around ₹3,000–₹6,000 but is time-consuming). Book flights 2–3 months in advance and watch for sales from IndiGo, Air India, and Vistara. Midweek flights (Tue–Thu) are typically 30–40% cheaper than weekend departures.

Accommodation Hacks

Skip the luxury resorts and stay at locally-run guesthouses and homestays. On Havelock, basic beach huts start at ₹800–₹1,200/night. On Neil Island, even ₹600/night gets you a clean room. Port Blair has budget hotels near Aberdeen Bazaar from ₹500/night. Book directly with the property to avoid aggregator markups.

Eating Well for Less

Local dhabas and small restaurants serve excellent meals for ₹100–₹200 per plate. Try the fresh fish thali, coconut-based curries, and local lobster (much cheaper than on the mainland). Carry snacks and water from Port Blair — prices double on smaller islands.

Free & Low-Cost Activities

Radhanagar Beach, Kalapathar Beach, the Elephant Beach trek, Chidiya Tapu sunset point, and the Corbyn's Cove beach are all free. The Cellular Jail entry is just ₹30 (₹10 for Indians). Snorkelling gear rental starts at ₹300–₹600. Government ferries between islands are significantly cheaper than private cruises (₹400–₹700 vs ₹1,200–₹1,800).

The Smart 4-Night Budget Itinerary

Day 1–2: Port Blair (Cellular Jail, Ross Island, North Bay — ₹1,500 for the boat combo). Day 3–4: Havelock (government ferry ₹700, stay in a hut, trek to Elephant Beach, watch sunset at Radhanagar). Day 5: Return to Port Blair and fly out. Total estimated cost: ₹12,000–₹15,000 per person excluding flights.
